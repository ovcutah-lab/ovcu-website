About OVCU

Utah Volleyball Built on Grass

The Outdoor Volleyball Club of Utah (OVCU) is the go-to community for competitive grass volleyball in the state. From first-timers to seasoned competitors, we run tournaments designed to challenge every level of play.

We host events across the Wasatch Front — Liberty Park, Sugar House, Provo, and more — with beautiful mountain backdrops and top-tier competition every weekend.

How It Works

Tournament Format

01
Register Your Team
Sign up through the registration form. Choose your division — Novice, BB, or AA — and secure your spot before it fills.
02
Check In at 8:30am
Arrive early, check in with your team, and get your bracket assignment. Pool play begins promptly after check-in.
03
Compete & Win
Battle through pool play into bracket competition. Winners earn OVCU bragging rights and prizes.
